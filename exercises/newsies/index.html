~ Man Stares Into Distance for Long Periods of Time ~

     Dale Pewman, a 32 year old man from Vermont, often finds himself staring off into scenic views. "It is only natural", says Dale. "Too many people are trying to define themselves, when really you are already defined by the cosmos." At this point, Dale trailed off as his gaze drifted back into the distance. We were unable to grab his attention for the better part of an hour, so we left him there. From what we are told Dale is still there sitting and staring into the distance, and there is no signs of him stopping.
