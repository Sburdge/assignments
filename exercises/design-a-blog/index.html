Today was a good day

   Today finishes the first official week of fullstack javascipt bootcamp at V school. I say today is a good day because after one week I still feel ahead of the content and am moving forward at a comfortable pace. I know that within the next week or two the content will get much heavier, but with this baseline knowledge I feel I am ready for it.

     So far the exercises and assignments they have had us do are very helpful. This week has been all about the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This blog is actually only made with HTML and CSS currently, but in the future I plan to make it much more reactive in addition to adding functionalities such as animations, scroll effects, forms, and additional posts, which will all be done though JavaScript logic.
